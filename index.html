About Me

Who are you?

My name is Nathan Tompkins. I'm a computer science student that loves graphic design. This is a simple website to house my stories and projects.

Aside from my course work, I work with computers to help people that don't really know what they're doing. I also do lots of graphic design, and I occasionally write. If you want me to work for you, then just get in touch. I have a few “internet accounts” that you can look at my creations on. In the end, though, those websites only have images and letters of stuff I've made. If you want the real deal, feel free to send me an email.

What is Anguna?

It's my name, Nathan, but each letter is replaced with the letter 13 spaces away. I know, "How fascinating," you say to yourself, but my Wu-Tang name wasn't very good, so here we are.

Do you update this website?

Yes, often, just not in obvious ways; I tweak little, insignificant things all the time. I'm not a consistant writer or artist or anything, really. If I have hurt you in this, please write out your grievances and send them to me in an email that I will (maybe not) read.
