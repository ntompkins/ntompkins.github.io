Biography

August 1, 2019 - Nathan Tompkins #biography

I've been working with computers for a long time, and my interest in them came about mostly through osmosis; I grew up around them and learned how to fix them by breaking them. I am currently interning in software R&D where I am working with Go, AWS, and Segment + analytics. At school, I work as a Production Assistant and Graphic Designer part time, and Assistant Resident Director for my residence hall full time. Outside of CS, I am very passionate about graphic design and music.
